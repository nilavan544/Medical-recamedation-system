About MyHealthMate

Your Health, Powered by AI

Welcome to MyHealthMate — an AI-powered platform designed to help you understand your health better. We believe that everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, and intelligent health insights. Whether you're feeling unwell or just curious, we're here to guide you.

Our Vision

We dream of a world where technology bridges the gap between medical knowledge and everyday people. MyHealthMate is our step toward that — using machine learning to make healthcare information simple, reliable, and available to all.

Who We Are

This platform is created by a passionate team led by Nilavan — a young AI/ML enthusiast, developer, and creator of the YouTube channel Nilavan Talks. Backed by a love for healthcare innovation and technology, this tool is designed with real people in mind.

Our Mission

We aim to assist users in identifying possible diseases based on symptoms using AI models, while also offering educational content. No more confusion or panic over health symptoms — get AI-backed predictions and guidance instantly.

How It Works

Enter your symptoms → Our system processes it using a trained ML model → You get predictions and suggestions. It's that easy. Behind the scenes, we use data-driven algorithms trained on thousands of symptom-disease patterns to ensure accurate results.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Disease predictions based on your symptoms
  • Easy-to-understand disease descriptions
  • Precaution tips, home remedies, dietary advice
  • Workout and lifestyle suggestions
  • And more updates coming soon!

Your Health, Our Priority

We care about your well-being. This platform is built to help — not replace — real medical advice. It’s a smart companion for your health journey, available anytime, anywhere.
